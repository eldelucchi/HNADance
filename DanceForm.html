Terms and Conditions


I understand that any guest of an HNA student must observe all school rules and

regulations as outlined in the HNA Parent/Student Handbook. The guest must bring

photo ID. HNA administration has the right to deny any guest access to the dance. Any

guest currently suspended or expelled from any public or private high school may not

attend.


I understand that students and guests are required to wear clothing which is appropriate

for the school environment. Dresses must be of a modest cut, not reveal undergarments,

and have a maximum length of 4 inches above the knee. All tops must cover the back,

both shoulders, and be long enough to reach the top of the beltline or waist. Students not

adhering to the HNA dress code may not be admitted or may be asked to leave.


I understand that students and guests are invited to participate in the merriment of

dancing in a way which is appropriate for the school environment. Students must dance

face to face, have space in between them, and remain upright. Any students or guests

dancing or behaving inappropriately will be asked to leave the dance and may not be

permitted to attend future dances. If a student is asked to leave the dance, parents will be

notified.
